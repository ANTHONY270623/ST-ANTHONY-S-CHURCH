About Fr. Sijo

Fr. Sijo has been serving as Assistant Vicar at St. Anthony's Church since 2017. He is dedicated to youth ministry and has established several programs to engage young people in the church community.

He is known for his inspiring homilies and compassionate pastoral care, particularly in ministering to the sick and elderly of the parish.
