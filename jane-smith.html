Responsibilities

As the Vice President of the Parish Council, Prakash assists the President in administrative duties and takes the lead in several key areas:

  • Heading the parish outreach committee
  • Organizing the annual charity drive
  • Coordinating with local community services
  • Filling in for the President when needed

About Prakash

Prakash has been deeply involved with St. Anthony's Church community outreach programs for over a decade. His background in community service has been invaluable in developing relationships between the church and local organizations.

He has been instrumental in establishing the parish's food bank program which now serves over 50 families weekly. Under his leadership, the annual charity fundraiser has doubled its contributions over the past three years.

Prakash is also involved in the church choir and leads the men's fellowship group every Thursday evening.
