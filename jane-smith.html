Responsibilities

As the Vice President of the Parish Council, Jane assists the President in administrative duties and takes the lead in several key areas:

  • Heading the parish outreach committee
  • Organizing the annual charity drive
  • Coordinating with local community services
  • Filling in for the President when needed

About Jane

Jane has been deeply involved with St. Anthony's Church community outreach programs for over a decade. Her background in social work has been invaluable in developing relationships between the church and local community organizations.

She has been instrumental in establishing the parish's food bank program which now serves over 50 families weekly. Under her leadership, the annual charity fundraiser has doubled its contributions over the past three years.

Jane is also involved in the church choir and leads the women's Bible study group every Tuesday evening.
